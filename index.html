About the Program

The PIAIC GenAI program is designed to equip students with cutting-edge skills in Generative AI technologies.

Our comprehensive curriculum covers the latest advancements in AI, focusing on generative models, natural language processing, and creative AI applications. Join us to be at the forefront of the AI revolution!

Key Topics Covered

  • Foundations of Machine Learning and Deep Learning
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Transformers
  • Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs)
  • Large Language Models (LLMs)
  • AI Ethics and Responsible AI Development
  • Practical Projects and Real-world Applications
